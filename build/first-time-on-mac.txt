Beacon — first time on this Mac
================================

macOS may block Beacon once because it isn’t from the App Store.
That’s normal. You only need to do this once.

1. Double-click Beacon in this window.
   It installs to your Applications folder.

2. If Mac says Beacon “can’t be opened”:
   • Open Applications
   • Right-click Beacon
   • Choose Open
   • Click Open again

3. Open Beacon anytime later:
   Press ⌘Space, type Beacon, press Enter.

Questions? Menu bar → Beacon → First open help…
